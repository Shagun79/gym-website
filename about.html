OUR STORY

Gym and Fitness was founded in 2002
as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality
gym equipment at great prices. Instead of being just another gym equipment
retailer, our founders wanted to be the best
in the industry and set their minds to doing so! Over
the last two decades Gym and Fitness has
grown into one of Australia’s largest
online fitness equipment retailers, helping thousands of customers
live longer, happier and healthier lives.

OUR VALUES

For 20 years helping customers reach their
fitness goals has been at the heart of what we do
and why we do it! We live and breathe our six core values and four brand promises
— which speak of our commitment to our customers, staff,
the industry and our business as a whole.
